KCOIN BLOCKCHAIN NETWORK

We are a blockchain platform that facilitates the launch of blockchain ecosystems, we provide a working private blockchain ran by Kellogg School of Business EMBA Graduates that includes wallets, master nodes, smart contract technology, rewards per transactions and APIs for easy integration to hybrid ecosystems. The Kcoin network is a worldwide collection of Kcoin nodes, each maintained by different people and organizations. The privacy of the network makes it fast, reliable and secure.
